📊 Music Store Data Analysis Project

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a Music Store database using PostgreSQL to uncover valuable business insights through SQL queries. The goal is to explore customer behavior, sales trends, music preferences, and revenue generation to support data-driven business decisions.


🗂 Dataset Description

The dataset represents a digital music store and contains information about customers, invoices, tracks, artists, albums, genres, and employees.

Table Structure

| Table Name | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Employee | Employee details and job positions |
| Customer | Customer information and demographics |
| Invoice | Purchase transactions made by customers |
| Invoice_Line | Details of tracks purchased in each invoice |
| Track | Information about songs and track duration |
| Album | Album details |
| Artist | Artist information |
| Genre | Music genre classifications |
| Playlist | Playlist information |
| Playlist_Track | Mapping between playlists and tracks |
| Media_Type | Audio format details |

📈 Analysis & Insights

1️⃣ Employee Analysis

Identified the senior-most employee based on job title hierarchy.

2️⃣ Sales Performance Analysis

Analyzed invoice data to determine:

• Countries generating the most invoices  
• Cities contributing the highest revenue  
• Top invoice totals across all transactions  

3️⃣ Customer Analysis

Determined:

• Best customers based on total spending  
• Customer purchase behavior patterns  
• Revenue contribution by individual customers  

4️⃣ Music Preference Analysis

Explored:

• Most popular music genres  
• Rock music listeners  
• Top-performing artists based on sales  

5️⃣ Advanced Revenue Analysis

Calculated:

• Customer spending on specific artists  
• Most popular genre in each country  
• Highest-spending customer by country  

6️⃣ Track Analysis

Identified songs with durations longer than the average track length.


📊 Key Insights

• A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue.  
• Rock music is among the most popular genres in the store.  
• Certain cities and countries generate substantially higher sales than others.  
• Top artists account for a large share of music purchases.  
• Customer preferences vary across different geographic regions.  
• Revenue patterns reveal opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.
